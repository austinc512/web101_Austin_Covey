Background and Education

My quest for knowledge has led me to the FullStack JavaScript Development Bootcamp at Austin Coding Academy. Expected to graduate in the fall of 2023, I have been immersing myself in the world of coding to sharpen my technical prowess.

Prior to this, I earned a Bachelor of Arts in Music from Texas State University, graduating in the summer of 2019. The pursuit of music has been a significant part of my life, and has allowed me to foster my creativity and personal expression.

Industry Experience in Tech

Prior to joining this bootcamp, I had the opportunity to work with Auctane, a cloud-based SaaS company that provides order management systems and shipping label solutions. From March 2021 to May 2023, I contributed my skills and expertise to their ShipStation and ShipEngine brands.

My tenure at Auctane began as a valuable member of the frontline support team at ShipStation. Over time, my dedication and passion for solving complex problems propelled me to the API support team, where I spent the last 11 months of my journey. This L2 support team is responsible for troubleshooting and customer inquiry related to ShipStation and ShipEngine's REST APIs.

During my time in the tech industry, I have gained proficiency in a wide range of tools including Postman, JIRA, Sumo Logic, Kibana, RedShift, GitHub, and New Relic.
